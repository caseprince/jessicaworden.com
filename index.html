Jessica C. Worden has a strong background in environmental law, green building, and sustainability procedures. She is knowledgeable in a variety of environmental topics including alternative energy, global warming, superfund sites, and brownfields. She is also versed in various environmental laws and regulations including the Clean Air and Clean Water acts, Texas state and local law, and the Texas Commission on
Environmental Quality.

Ms. Worden founded TerraNova Auditing & Consulting in 2009. Terranova uses a multi-pronged approach to address our current environmental problems, embodying Ms. Worden's tenacity, energy, and passion for the environment.

In late 2010, Jessica started her own law firm (www.wordenlawfirm.com). This boutique firm focuses on criminal, family, and probate work. Jessica strives to provide excellent client communications and effective and efficient results.
